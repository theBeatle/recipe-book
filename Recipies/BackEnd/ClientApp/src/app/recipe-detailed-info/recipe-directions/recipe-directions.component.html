Directions

  1. Ek.Bowl Me Cornflour Ley.Usey Acche Sey Ghol Ready Kar Ley.
  2. Without Lumps..Fir Ek Pan Ley.Usme Sugar Daal Dey ,1/2cup Water Daal Kr Bs Sugar Dissolved Hone TK.Pakai..Then Add Cornflour Liquid.
  3. Usey Bilkul Low Flame Pr Rakhe Aor Lagatar Chalte Rhe.Jb Vo Thoda Thick Ho Jaey..TB 2tbsp Ghee Add Kre Fir Chalte Rhe.
  4. Isi Tarah Aapko Fir 2baar 3_3 Tbsp Ghee Chalte Huey Add Karna H.
  5. Fir Colour Add Kre .1lemon Juice Add Kre..Fir Isey TB TK Chaley Jb TK Ye Transparent N Ho Jaey..
  6. Or Deakhne Me Lgne Lge Ki Ab Ye Jum Sakta H
  7. Fir Ek Plate Me Ghee Greece Kr Isey Acche Sey Spread Kar Dey.
  8. Fir 1 Hour K Ley Room Temperature Or Chod Dey
  9. Uske Baad Aap Apne Choice K Shape K According Cut Kre.
  10. Ho Gya Ready
